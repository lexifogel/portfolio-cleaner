Hobbies

Biking

This spring, I got into road biking. Through the summer and fall, I went on daily excursions to local parks to sweat my stress away. In Ann Arbor, my favorite biking trail is from Gallup Park through Matthei Bontanical Gardens. At home, I ride the Schuylkill River Trail, Chester Valley Trail, and more. See my activities here!


Hiking & Walking

At Michigan and beyond, I love to explore my local environment on long walks through nature. Some of my favorite areas to pass through include the local neighborhoods and the Nichols Arboretum, but the best adventures are usually finding somewhere new!


Design

Photography has always been another passion of mine. Recently, I also have been getting into graphic design. I am currently taking a graphic design course, which I hope will allow me to combine these two passions in novel ways.
